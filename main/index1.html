About Kerala Tourism

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a tropical paradise in southern India. With its lush landscapes, serene backwaters, and rich cultural heritage, Kerala offers a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers.

Popular Destinations

Munnar

Picturesque hill station known for its tea plantations and stunning landscapes.

Alappuzha

Venice of the East, famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises.

Wayanad

Lush green hills, wildlife sanctuaries, and adventure activities.

Thrissur

Cultural capital of Kerala, home to the famous Thrissur Pooram festival.

Thekkady

Wildlife sanctuary and spice plantations in the Western Ghats.

Kumarakom

Serene backwater destination with luxury resorts and bird sanctuary.
